## In one paragraph

MuSeRC is the Russian SuperGLUE benchmark for multi-sentence reading comprehension. As
a national AI-Academy Olympiad project (2nd place, age 17), I improved on the published
state of the art by ensembling transformer models, engineering features from natural
language inference signals, and integrating auxiliary datasets — reaching 0.867 F1a on
the related RuCoS task and a +2% F1a gain on MuSeRC.

## Why it still matters to me

It was my first end-to-end research loop — error analysis, hypothesis, experiment,
repeat — and the reason I chose ML as a career rather than a hobby.
